About

My Story

Hi, my name is Supriya, an UX/UI Designer. I have a background in Civil Engineering with specialization in Structural Design engineering.

With 12 years of experience in civil engineering, I have developed strong problem-solving skills,which I now apply to creating intuitive and engaging user experiences. My engineering background has given me a solid foundation in conceptual, structural, and user-centered design.

Born and raised in India, I bring a diverse perspective to my work. I have a deep passion for history and nature, which inspires my design process. I love yoga and trekking, and these activities help me stay grounded and creative.

Alongside my engineering degree, I have completed the CareerFoundry UX/UI Design Course, making me well-versed in app development, wire-framing, and prototyping. With this specialized skill set, I am able to create intuitive and beautiful UX/UI designs.

I thrive on connecting with people and making new friends, and I bring this enthusiasm for connection to my design work, always focusing on the end user.
